About Us

Company Profile

SUNSEMI is a professional manufacturer of FCOB LED tape lights, dedicated to developing and producing high-quality lighting products.

Company Overview

SUNSEMI is a professional manufacturer specializing in FCOB (Flip Chip On Board) LED tape lights.

Our products feature high CRI (Ra95+), dotless illumination, and ultra-thin design, trusted by professionals in commercial facilities, residential spaces, hotels, and retail stores worldwide.

Under our quality-first policy, we deliver the best lighting solutions through strict quality control and continuous technological innovation.
